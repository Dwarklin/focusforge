Welcome to Focus Forge!

Your AI-powered productivity companion. This tutorial will guide you through all the features.

1

Adding Tasks

Start by adding your tasks:

  • Click on the "My Tasks" tab
  • Fill in the task name
  • Select priority (High, Medium, Low)
  • Set duration in minutes
  • Click "Add Task" or the + button
2

Managing Your Task List

Organize your tasks easily:

  • Check the box to mark tasks complete
  • Click the pencil icon to edit details
  • Use the trash icon to delete tasks
  • Tasks are automatically saved
3

Generating Your Schedule

Let AI optimize your day:

  • Switch to "AI Schedule" tab
  • Set your work hours and break preferences
  • Click "Generate AI Schedule"
  • The system will prioritize tasks and insert breaks
  • Mark tasks complete directly from the schedule
!

Pro Tips

  • Use the floating + button to quickly add tasks
  • High priority tasks are scheduled first
  • Take advantage of the built-in break system
  • Your data is saved automatically
  • Refresh the page to start fresh
